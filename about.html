About VR1 Site

The VR1 site was founded by Vishal, who started with an agenda to create a movie streaming site. Over the course of four months, Vishal and his team worked tirelessly, and with immense leadership, they succeeded in developing the movie streaming site within 16 weeks. Following this initial success, in collaboration with his team and leadership, Vishal took it up a notch by adding series, games, and workout interfaces to ensure users could stay entertained and fit.

Vishal began as a front-end developer, but through his dedication and hard work, he transformed the website within 24 weeks, taking GUI and front-end development to the next level.
